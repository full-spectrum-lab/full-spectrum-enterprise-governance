# Human Review Workflow

Full Spectrum enterprise governance assumes that high-risk outcomes should not end at AI output.

## Minimal review chain

```text
AI output
  -> inspection
  -> risk surfaced
  -> review requested
  -> human owner checks evidence, rule source, and impact
  -> final enterprise-owned decision
```

## Typical review owners

Depending on the scenario, review may involve:

- customer-service supervisor
- QA lead
- merchant operations owner
- after-sales policy owner
- compliance or risk-control owner

## What human review should answer

1. Was the AI authorized to make that commitment?
2. Did the available evidence support the answer?
3. Did the system miss a state change?
4. Was user trust already being damaged?
5. What should be recorded for later training or rule correction?

## Boundary

Human review is not a decorative fallback.

It is the accountability re-entry point for high-impact or unclear cases.
